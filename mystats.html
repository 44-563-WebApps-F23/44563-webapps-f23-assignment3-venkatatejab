Chicken Biryani

The succulent pieces of chicken, marinated in a medley of spices, are layered with fragrant, long-grain basmati rice cooked to perfection. The symphony of ingredients, including aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, cardamom, and saffron, creates a rich and complex flavor profile that is both comforting and exciting to the palate.
